Welcome to Harshu Apartments  

Started in 2014 Harshu Apartments  had 1 vision which never changed. Our vision is providing the guest with an experience. Not just a simple night away. Everything we do is to create the WOW feeling. To think outside the box and make you feel very special.

About Us

To create this experience we beautifully design our apartments, provide them with all the luxuryequipment and locate them in the city centre of hyderabad.
Our Harshu Apartments are available for leisure and business. Suitable for 2 till 12 guests in the same apartment. With all the beautiful monuments and stores on walking distance. Close by the apartments Harshu Apartments  serves a delisious breakfast buffet with fresh orange juice, fresh pan cakes, croissants, yoghurt, cornflakes, Nespresso coffe, tea and more.
